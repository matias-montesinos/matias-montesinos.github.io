About

Welcome to my academic website. I study the physics of protoplanetary disks, planet formation, and tidal disruption events using numerical simulations and theoretical models.

Research

My research focuses on the hydrodynamics of accretion disks, the processes that regulate planetary growth and migration, and the interaction between stars, disks, and planets. I use high-resolution simulations with codes such as FARGO3D to investigate gas dynamics, shocks, and feedback mechanisms.
